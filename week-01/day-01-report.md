### 8 Stages of Storytelling
1. A character in a zone of comfort
2. But they want something
3. Entered in unfamiliar situation
4. Adapt to it
5. Get what they want
6. Pay heavy price for it
7. Then return to familiar situation
8. Having changed

### 3 Stages of Video Production
1. **Pre-production** — Planning, scripting, shooting preparation
2. **Production** — Shooting, action phase
3. **Post-production** — Editing, polishing, final delivery
